ChromeAppLauncher

The best & quick access to Google™ Services and Websites APIs


Version: 24.12.25 Updated: Dec 25, 2024 Size: 1.20MiB Languages: 3+

Workflow Optimization

Optimize your workflow by letting you open your most commonly-used websites directly from the toolbar.

Easy Access

Packed with more than 1,000 shortcuts. Open with just a click or keyboard shortcut.

Customizable

Personalize shortcuts, layouts, and appearance. Full dark theme support included.

Secure Sync

Browser-based sync ensures your settings follow you. Data is never sent to external systems.

Note on Permissions

To provide full functionality, the extension requires permissions for:

  • Automatically fetching icons for manual site additions.
  • Replacing default launchers for seamless integration.
  • Ensuring Google account synchronization.
  • Triggering action-based shortcuts (e.g., 'Gmail send this').

Installation Guide

1
Open your preferred browser.
2
Navigate to chrome://extensions/ (or equivalent for your browser).
3
Enable Developer Mode using the toggle in the top right.
4
Click Load unpacked and select the extension folder.
5
Extension is loaded! Enjoy the new experience.
